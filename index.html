About
Me

I am Manon, a Front-End Web Developer based in Toronto. I finished a nine-week bootcamp program at HackerYou in March 2018. My background is in Graphic Design and learning how languages and libraries such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React are used to build the websites I designed is very exciting!

The challenge of putting all the pieces together is what makes me excited about being a Front-End-Web Developer and I look forward to solving a lot more of these puzzles, whilst combining my Graphic Design with my coding skills, and getting inspired by the development community here in Toronto.
